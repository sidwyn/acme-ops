# Expense Policy

## Purpose
Provide guidance for submitting, approving, and reimbursing business expenses.

## Policy
- Submit expenses within 30 days of purchase.
- Attach itemized receipts for all claims.
- Manager approval required for reimbursements.

## Exceptions
- Travel expenses may be submitted within 60 days.
- Pre-approval required for expenses above $1,000.
- Contact Finance for policy clarification.
