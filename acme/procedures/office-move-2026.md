# Office Move 2026 Procedure

## Scope
This procedure outlines the steps required to plan and execute the 2026 office relocation.

## Timeline
- 6 months out: Confirm lease dates and budget.
- 3 months out: Finalize floor plan and vendors.
- 1 month out: Communicate move details to staff.

## Execution
- Schedule movers and IT support.
- Label equipment and assign responsibilities.
- Complete final walkthrough and handover.
